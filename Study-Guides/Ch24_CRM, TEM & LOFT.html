Crew Resource Management (CRM)
Definition · Objectives · Skills focus · Tenerife · Foundations · CRM vs flying skills
CRM Definition & Focus
CRM DEFINITION
Effective use of ALL available resources to ensure safe and efficient flight operations while reducing errors and stress.
Resources Include:
PilotsCabin CrewATC Aircraft SystemsChecklists SOPsGround Personnel
DGCA Key Point: CRM is NOT primarily about flying skills. It focuses on cognitive and interpersonal skills.
CRM focuses on:
Cognitive
Situational awareness · Problem solving · Decision making
Interpersonal
Communication · Leadership · Teamwork · Coordination
CRM — Why It Became Essential
🧠
CRM Foundations
1. Errors are NORMAL · 2. Non-punitive culture · 3. Learning organisation
CRM Objectives & Who Needs It
CRM Objectives:
✓Reduce human error
✓Improve teamwork
✓Improve decision making
✓Improve situational awareness
✓Prevent accidents and incidents
CRM Training Required For:
PilotsCabin Crew ATCOsEngineers DispatchersAll Aviation Personnel

LOFT — Line Oriented Flight Training
Realistic simulator training · NOT an examination · CRM/TEM practice
LOFT — What It Is & What It Is NOT
LOFT DEFINITION
Realistic simulator training based on actual airline operations.
Scenarios come from accident reports, incident reports, and operational experience.
LOFT Characteristics:
✓Real flight scenarios
✓Full crew participation
✓Real-time decision making
✓Communication & leadership exercises
✓No deliberate training interruptions
LOFT IS:
✓A training tool
✓For practising CRM & TEM skills
✓A safe environment to make mistakes
LOFT is NOT:
✗An examination
✗A performance check on individuals
✗A pass/fail assessment
DGCA Key Point: If weaknesses are found during LOFT, additional training is provided. There should be NO stigma, NO punishment, NO recrimination.

⚠️ Top DGCA Exam Traps — Chapter 24
🪤
CRM is NOT about flying skills
CRM focuses on COGNITIVE skills (SA, decision making) and INTERPERSONAL skills (communication, leadership, teamwork) — NOT technical flying ability.
🪤
LOFT is NOT an examination
LOFT = Line Oriented Flight Training = training tool only. It should NEVER be used to check individual performance. If weaknesses found, provide additional training with no stigma.
🪤
GPWS = L-H interface (hardware), NOT L-E
GPWS is a hardware device (L-H). Weather is L-E. Students mix these up because GPWS warns about terrain/environment, but it is itself a hardware instrument.
🪤
Checklist = L-S (Software), NOT L-H
Checklist is a SOFTWARE item (procedure/document). It goes in L-S. The aircraft instrument panel (hardware) is L-H.
🪤
TEM does NOT eliminate threats/errors
TEM recognizes that threats and errors are NORMAL and UNAVOIDABLE. TEM aims to RECOGNIZE, MANAGE and MINIMIZE consequences — not eliminate them.
🪤
Assertiveness ≠ Aggression
Assertiveness = Confident, respectful communication of safety concerns. NOT arrogance or aggression. Not assertive = remaining silent when danger is recognized.
