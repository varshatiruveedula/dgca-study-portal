Power to Exempt + Official Gazette

Under Section 3, the Central Government may exempt any aircraft/person/class from provisions of the Act, or modify applicability. Any such exemption MUST be published in the Official Gazette — the government's official publication for laws, rules, notifications, exemptions and appointments.

Power to Detain Aircraft (Sec 8)

Authorized authority may detain if ANY of these apply:
  • (a) Flight may endanger persons/property
  • (b) Necessary to ensure compliance with Act
  • (c) Necessary to prevent violation

Slaughter / Filth Near Aerodrome

🚫 Prohibited Within 10 km of Aerodrome Reference Point
  • Slaughtering or flaying animals
  • Depositing rubbish, filth, or obnoxious matter
Penalty
Up to 3 years imprisonment
OR ₹1 crore fine — OR BOTH

Wreck & Salvage (Sec 9)

Aircraft wreck/salvage provisions are similar to the Merchant Shipping Act. The owner is entitled to a reasonable salvage reward.

Good Faith & Bar of Suits (Sec 17 & 18)

Sec 17 — Bar of Suits
No civil suit for trespass or nuisance due to aircraft flying at reasonable height.
Sec 18 — Good Faith Protection
No legal action against persons acting in good faith under the Act.
Aircraft Rules, 1937 — Topic Groups
CLICK TO EXPAND EACH TOPIC GROUP
🛫 Registration & Nationality (Rules 2, 5, 30, 33) ▼
  • Rule 2 Aircraft possesses nationality of the State in whose register it is entered
  • Rule 5 Aircraft shall not fly unless: (a) Registered (b) Displays nationality & registration marks
  • Rule 5A No Indian aircraft shall leave India without permission · No aircraft shall fly to territory declared prohibited
  • Rule 30 Category A (Indian) = Indian citizens, companies, Govt · Category B (Foreign-linked) = foreign residents/businesses in India
  • Rule 33 Change of ownership must be notified to DGCA
👨‍✈️ Licensing & Personnel (Rules 6, 6A–6C, 38, 38A, 39A, 39B) ▼
  • Rule 6 Aircraft shall carry prescribed LICENSED personnel
  • Rule 6A Pilot shall not fly aircraft type NOT included in licence rating
  • Rule 6B Pilot MAY fly unendorsed type for licence extension/training
  • Rule 6C DGCA may authorise test flights on aircraft not in rating
  • Rule 38 Licensing Authority = Central Government
  • Rule 38A Private aircraft → PPL or higher · Public Transport → CPL/ATPL · Navigator needed if flight >600 NM without nav aids · Flight Radio Telephone Operator needed when radio carried
  • Rule 39A Disqualification reasons: alcohol/drug addiction, criminal conduct, fraudulent/tampered licence
  • Rule 39B Medical standards are mandatory
  • Rule 40 Licence holder shall sign their licence
  • Rule 41 Proof of competency required · Rule 41A DGCA may conduct tests & examinations
  • Rule 42 Expired 2–3 yrs → Skill test + Air Regs · Expired >3 yrs → Complete exams again
  • Rule 44 Foreign aircraft personnel licensed under state of registry
  • Rule 45 Foreign licences may be validated
📄 Documents & Checklists (Rules 7, 7A, 7B) ▼
  • Rule 7 Aircraft shall carry valid documents
  • Rule 7A No aircraft entering India shall carry persons WITHOUT valid passports
  • Rule 7B Must carry: Cockpit Checklist + Emergency Checklist — must be readily accessible to pilot
🔫 Arms, Security & Prohibited Areas (Rules 8, 8A, 12) ▼
  • Rule 8 Cannot carry Arms, Ammunition, Explosives, Military stores — without WRITTEN PERMISSION
  • Rule 8A Passengers and baggage may be searched before boarding
  • Rule 12 Aircraft shall NOT fly over prohibited areas · If inadvertently enters: give distress signal → land at nearest aerodrome → do not descend/fly further
Examples of Prohibited Areas: Rashtrapati Bhavan · Kalpakkam Nuclear Area · Mathura Refineries
✈️ Airworthiness & Conditions for Flight (Rules 15, 15A, 50, 52) ▼
  • Rule 15 Aircraft shall have: Valid C of A · Proper maintenance · Compliance with C of A conditions · Required documents onboard
  • Rule 15A Drone >250g requires: Unique Identification Number (UIN)
  • Rule 50 Certificate of Airworthiness issued/renewed by DGCA
  • Rule 52 Safety modifications / repairs require approval
🍺 Conduct, Alcohol & Safety Rules (Rules 21–24B) ▼
  • Rule 21 No dangerous flying — no operation causing danger to persons/property
  • Rule 22 No assault, intimidation or threat to crew
  • Rule 23 No acts endangering safety or discipline onboard
  • Rule 24 12-Hour Bottle-to-Throttle — No alcohol within 12 hrs before flight · No flying while impaired
  • Rule 24A Mentally disordered persons require medical certification + conditions
  • Rule 24B Prisoners may be carried ONLY with written permission
  • Rule 24C Animals/birds/reptiles require permission
💡 Remember
12-Hour Bottle-to-Throttle — Rule 24 — most examined rule on alcohol
⛽ Fuelling, Smoking & Housing (Rules 25, 25A, 25B) ▼
  • Rule 25 Aircraft shall display smoking notices
  • Rule 25A Fuelling: Min 15m from building · No smoking/open flame within 30m · Turbojet: stop fuelling if jet exhaust within 43m
  • Rule 25B Hangars housing aircraft with petroleum must be fire safe and well ventilated
🧑‍✈️ Age Limits (Rules 28, 28A) ▼
RuleAge LimitContext

🌐 Modern Rules — SMS, PEDs, Sonic Boom (Rules 29A–29D) ▼
  • Rule 29 No interference with crew
  • Rule 29A Civil aircraft shall NOT create sonic boom over India
  • Rule 29B Portable Electronic Devices — internet via Wi-Fi allowed when approved
  • Rule 29C DGCA adopts ICAO Convention and Annexes
  • Rule 29D Organizations must establish Safety Management System (SMS)
📁 Operations, Manuals & DGCA Directions (Rules 133A, 134, 140, 140B, 140C) ▼
  • Rule 133A DGCA may issue: CARs · AICs · AIPs · NOTAM-related directions
  • Rule 134 Scheduled Air Transport requires Government permission
  • Rule 140 Operators must comply with engineering and safety requirements
  • Rule 140B Operations Manual must include: Responsibilities · Crew requirements · Normal procedures · Emergency procedures · Minimum safe altitudes
  • Rule 140C Route Guide must contain: Navigation aids · Alternate aerodromes · Weather minima · Fuel requirements
  • Rule 161 Penalties for contraventions
📝 Logbooks (Rule 67A, 67B) ▼
  • Rule 67A Maintain personal logbook · Entries in ink · Preserve minimum 5 years
  • Rule 67B False entries in logbooks — prohibited
🚫 Dropping, Parachute, Sonic Boom, Prohibited Flights ▼
  • Rule 26 No dropping articles from aircraft — Exceptions: Emergency fuel jettison · Emergency cargo drop · Flower petals (with permission)
  • Rule 27 No carriage of persons in unauthorized parts of aircraft
