Key Takeoff & Landing Rules
🚫
Never take off or land without ATC clearance — even if runway appears clear ★
1️⃣
Only ONE aircraft may be cleared to land on runway at a time (exception: military formation)
📋
Cockpit checks before line-up — not on the runway, to minimise occupancy time
⚡
Immediate takeoff = taxi onto runway + take off in one continuous movement (no stopping)
🛬
Landing has priority over departing aircraft on same / intersecting runway
🔄
Backtrack: notify ATC BEFORE commencing taxi
🧠
Separation Memory
Preceding aircraft: Crossed end of runway OR Started a turn OR All landing aircraft clear
Runway Incursion Actions
🚨 Vehicle/aircraft enters runway after clearance issued:
→
Inform aircraft immediately (obstruction + location)
→
If before takeoff roll: Cancel takeoff clearance
→
If landing clearance issued: GO AROUND ★
🧭 Aircraft lost on manoeuvring area:
1
STOP immediately ★
2
Notify ATS with last known position
!
If on RUNWAY: vacate if possible, then stop
Taxi Clearance Rules
✓
Must be concise · avoid collisions · avoid runway incursions
★
If route crosses a runway, ATC must explicitly say: "Cross Runway XX" or "Hold Short of Runway XX"
✓
Runway vacated = entire aircraft beyond holding position (not just nose or mains)

⚠️ Top Exam Traps
🪤
Medical (MEDEVAC) is Priority 4 — ABOVE VVIP (Priority 5)
VVIP appears important but MEDEVAC outranks it. Emergency is #1, then military fuel constraints, then scramble, THEN Medical, then VVIP.
🪤
Startup clearance = Clearance Delivery (not Tower, not Ground)
Startup clearance is issued by Clearance Delivery position. Tower = runway + airborne. Ground = taxiways + aprons. Clearance Delivery = startup + ATC route clearance.
🪤
Long final = MORE THAN 4 NM, up to 8 NM (not 2-4 or 10+)
Long final = >4 NM up to 8 NM from threshold. Exactly 4 NM is NOT long final. More than 8 NM would be extended final only.
🪤
RWYCC 1 = POOR (lowest number = worst). RWYCC 5 = GOOD (highest = best)
Counterintuitive: higher code number = better braking. Code 5 = Good (μ≥0.40). Code 1 = Poor (μ≤0.25).
🪤
Runway vacated = ENTIRE aircraft beyond holding position (not just nose/mains)
Runway not vacated until the ENTIRE aircraft — including tail — has crossed the holding position marking. Partial clearing is not "vacated."
🪤
Lights must be reactivatable 1 HOUR before arrival (not 15 or 30 min)
If aerodrome lights are turned off (no expected ops), they must be capable of reactivation at least 1 HOUR before expected arrival — not 15 or 30 minutes.
