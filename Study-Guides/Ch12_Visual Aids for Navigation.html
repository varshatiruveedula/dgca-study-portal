Displaced Threshold vs Normal Threshold
Exam question: Arrows before the threshold mark = Displaced threshold. The area before displaced threshold cannot be used for landing but CAN be used for take-off roll and taxi.
Runway Holding Position Marking
Golden Rule: 4 solid + 4 dashed yellow lines. Aircraft STOPS before crossing the SOLID lines. Dashed side faces the runway.

⚠️ Top DGCA Exam Traps
🪤
PAPI side — LEFT of runway
PAPI is on the LEFT side of the runway (when looking toward the threshold). Frequently asked trap.
🪤
Apron Safety Line = RED (India only)
Apron safety lines are RED in India. Don't confuse with taxiway (Yellow) or runway (White).
🪤
Aiming Point ≠ Touchdown Zone
Aiming Point = big blocks = pilot visually aims. TDZ = small paired bars = where wheels touch. Different markings!
🪤
Location sign = Yellow on BLACK
Location = Yellow on Black ("black square = you are there"). Direction/Info = Black on Yellow. Opposite!
🪤
CAT II/III side rows = RED, not white
CAT I has NO side rows. CAT II/III has RED side rows (270m). The centreline is white in both.
🪤
Clearway vs Stopway
Clearway = AIR (aircraft climbs over it, cannot land/stop on it). Stopway = GROUND (aircraft stops on it for rejected takeoff).
