⚠️ Top Exam Traps
🪤
NOTAM(R) = Replacement NOT Revision
NOTAM(R) means REPLACEMENT — it completely replaces a previous NOTAM. It does NOT mean "Revision." This is the most common trap in this chapter.
🪤
AIC is published by DGCA, not AAI
All AIS publications (AIP, NOTAM, AIP Amdt, AIP Supplement) are published by AAI. AIC is the only exception — published by DGCA.
🪤
AIP Supplement = Yellow, not white
AIP Supplement pages are preferably YELLOW. This distinguishes them from normal AIP pages and makes them easy to identify and remove when expired.
🪤
AIRAC = 28 days · Distribution = 42 days
The AIRAC CYCLE is 28 days. But distribution happens 42 days before effective date. Major changes need 56 days. Users receive 28 days before. Don't confuse these four numbers.
🪤
SNOWTAM ≠ ASHTAM — completely different
SNOWTAM = runway surface (snow/ice/slush/frost/water). ASHTAM = volcanic ash/activity. Never mix these in the exam.
🪤
Series C = Domestic (not international)
Series A = International >2hrs. Series B = International 30min–2hrs. Series C = Domestic ONLY. Series D = Military aerodromes.
