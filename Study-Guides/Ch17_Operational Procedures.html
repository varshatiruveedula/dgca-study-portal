Air Operator Certificate (AOC)
AOC = Permission to run an airline. Authorises an operator to conduct specified commercial air transport operations.
✓
Issued by State of the Operator
✓
State where principal place of business exists
✓
Other contracting states shall recognise AOC if it meets Annex 6 standards
Before granting AOC, State must verify:
✓Adequate organisation
✓Training programs
✓Flight operations
✓Ground handling
✓Maintenance arrangements
✓Capability for safe operations
AOC stays valid only if operator continues to maintain original certification standards and continuous surveillance requirements.

Approach Ban Policy — Most Asked DGCA Topic

❌ Cannot Commence Approach

PIC shall NOT start instrument approach if reported RVR/Visibility is below applicable minimum

❌ Approach NOT Continued if aircraft is:

• Above 1000 ft over aerodrome
• Not yet in final approach segment

✅ Approach MAY Continue if:

• Aircraft has entered final approach segment
• Descended below 1000 ft above aerodrome

✅ Landing Allowed Only If:

Required visual reference established at DA/H or MDA/H AND maintained
Key threshold: 1000 ft above aerodrome is the decision point. Once below 1000 ft or in final approach — approach may continue to DA/MDA.

⚠️ Common DGCA Exam Traps
🪤
FDR vs CVR retention
FDR = 25 hours. CVR = 2 hours (standard). New large aircraft CVR post-2022 = 25 hours too.
🪤
Special VFR for commercial air transport
Special VFR is NOT permitted for commercial air transport aeroplanes. Very common trap.
🪤
Final Reserve — Recip vs Turbine
Reciprocating = 45 min. Turbine = 30 min. Piston engines need more reserve due to lower efficiency at altitude.
🪤
ACAS II vs GPWS threshold
ACAS II = turbine >5700 kg OR >19 pax. GPWS (windshear) = turbojet >5700 kg OR >9 pax. Different passenger thresholds!
🪤
Positioning vs Deadheading
Both mean the same thing. Positioning = Deadheading. Transfer of non-operating crew.
🪤
ELT threshold
Every aircraft = 1 automatic ELT. Aircraft with >19 pax = 2 ELTs (1 must be automatic). Life rafts = 2 survival ELTs.
